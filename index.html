About Me

Hi I am Armie Jane Timbal My life is full of ups and downs, growing in a family with strict father and brothers is different. Hits different, so many No’s than Yes. Sometimes it feels like I don’t have freedom to choose what I want to be because they think I can’t do it but I am striving without them knowing. I am thriving under pressure but it ignites me. It is not easy that the world is looking upon you, they want to see you shine, they want you to be these and that but sometimes, you ask yourself- can I really make it? It is so ironic how others believes in you but you are the one who question yourself. And that leads to pressuring yourself. Having a soldier father, a coast guard officer brother and a future officer soldier brother is making my nerve-racking. The pressure is in me, can I really make them proud? Well, I am beyond grateful for them to trust my capabilities but I think I am pushing my self to the extent that it consumes me.
